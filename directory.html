If you live in Hackney, work here or are visiting, join us. Thanks to Create Jobs for the design work.

About the social enterprise sector in Hackney

There are at least 300 emerging and bona fide social enterprises operating in or from Hackney: co-operatives, CICs, community businesses, trading not-for-profits and social purpose trading arms. These range from tiny entrepreneurial luncheon-clubs to national enterprises such as HCT Group, who run bus routes and community transport all around the UK, with a turnover of £43.7 million.
